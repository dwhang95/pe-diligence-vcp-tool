# Prompt: Synergy Analysis

Generate the Synergy Analysis section for the PE ops diligence brief.
This section is most relevant for add-on acquisitions, mergers, and platform build strategies.

## Input Context
- Company: {company_name}
- Description: {description}
- Industry: {industry}
- EV range: {ev_range}
- Deal type: {deal_type}
- Context notes: {context_notes}
- Research findings: {research_summary}

## Instructions

Analyze synergy potential for this transaction. Be directional: call out which synergies are real vs. speculative. Do not dress up unlikely synergies with optimistic language.

**1. Revenue Synergies**
Identify specific cross-sell, market access, or pricing synergies.
Assign confidence: **High / Medium / Low**
Justify in one sentence. Note any execution dependencies.

**2. Cost Synergies**
Identify overlap in procurement, G&A, headcount, facilities, or technology stack.
Assign confidence: **High / Medium / Low**
Justify in one sentence. Flag one-time integration costs that offset synergies.

**3. Integration Risks**
Name the 2–3 most serious risks. Be specific.
- Culture and leadership alignment
- IT system integration complexity and timeline
- Customer/contract retention during transition
- Key-man dependencies that complicate integration

**4. Timeline & Phasing**
- Day 1 must-do items (before or immediately after close)
- 0–6 months: stabilization priorities
- 6–18 months: realistic synergy capture targets (with dollar range if estimable)

**5. Recommended Deal Structure Considerations**
- Earn-out design considerations (if synergies are contingent on seller cooperation)
- Management retention equity structure
- Integration governance: dedicated PMO vs. embedded resources

## Format

Lead with a one-sentence characterization of the overall synergy opportunity (e.g., "Cost synergies are real but modest; revenue synergies are speculative at this stage.").
Write as flowing analysis with bolded sub-headers for each of the 5 areas.
Length: 400–550 words.
