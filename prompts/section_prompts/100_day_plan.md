# Prompt: 100-Day Plan Outline Starter

Generate the 100-Day Plan Outline section for the PE ops diligence brief.

## Input Context
- Company: {company_name} | Industry: {industry} | EV: {ev_range}
- Description: {description}
- Risk flags identified: {risk_flags_summary}
- Value creation levers identified: {vc_levers_summary}
- Research findings: {research_summary}

## Instructions

Generate a 100-day plan outline that is specific to this company — not a generic template. The priorities should flow directly from the risk flags and value creation levers identified earlier.

**Three non-negotiable principles:**
1. Days 1–30 are for listening, not fixing. Premature action destroys trust and misses root causes.
2. Quick wins (Days 30–60) must be both visible AND impactful — not just easy.
3. Board reporting cadence must be established by Day 60.

**What makes a 100-day plan specific (not generic):**
- Reference the actual risk flags identified (e.g., "Validate key-man dependency hypothesis — who really owns the {company_name} customer relationships?")
- Reference specific value creation levers (e.g., "Launch working capital diagnostic: pull AR aging, inventory turns data by SKU")
- Name the stakeholder groups specific to this industry and company type
- Call out data requests that are company/industry-specific, not boilerplate

**Phase Structure:**

### Days 1–30: Listen & Diagnose
- Listening tour priorities (who to talk to and in what order)
- Key data requests to issue in week 1
- Hypothesis validation agenda (which risk flags need immediate attention)
- Relationship priorities (management, board, key customers/suppliers)

### Days 31–60: Align & Plan
- Priority workstreams to formally launch
- Quick wins to execute (with owner and timeline)
- VCP v1 draft and alignment with management
- Governance setup (operating cadence, KPI dashboard, reporting)

### Days 61–100: Execute & Report
- First deliverables from priority workstreams
- KPI baseline established
- Board reporting cadence running
- 100-day retrospective and Year 1 plan handoff

## Format
```
## 5. 100-Day Plan Outline Starter

### Days 1–30: Listen & Diagnose
- {Bullet 1}
- {Bullet 2}
- {Bullet 3}
- {Bullet 4}
- {Bullet 5}

### Days 31–60: Align & Plan
...

### Days 61–100: Execute & Report
...
```
